# Design System — Business Growth Diagnostic

## Inspiration
Dark SaaS landing page: near-black background, bold display typography, orange/amber accent color, clean sections with strong visual hierarchy. Analytical, professional, modern — not hypey.

## Colors
- Background primary: `#0a0a0a` (near black)
- Background secondary: `#111111`
- Background card: `#161616`
- Background elevated: `#1c1c1c`
- Border: `#2a2a2a`
- Accent primary: `#f97316` (orange-500)
- Accent hover: `#ea6c0a`
- Accent muted: `#7c3a1a` (dark orange tint)
- Text primary: `#f5f5f5`
- Text secondary: `#a3a3a3`
- Text muted: `#666666`
- Success: `#22c55e`
- Danger: `#ef4444`

## Typography
- Display: `Inter`, weight 700-900, tracking tight
- Body: `Inter`, weight 400-500
- Mono: `JetBrains Mono` or `monospace` for data/metrics

## Spacing
- Section padding: `py-24` or `py-32`
- Card padding: `p-6` or `p-8`
- Container max-width: `max-w-6xl mx-auto px-6`

## Components
- Cards: dark bg `#161616`, border `#2a2a2a`, subtle hover border glow in orange
- Buttons primary: orange bg, white text, slight radius
- Badges: small pill, orange or muted
- Stats: large bold numbers, muted label below
- Section labels: small caps orange text above headings
- Dividers: `#2a2a2a` 1px

## Style Notes
- No gradients except subtle dark-to-dark or orange glow behind hero headline
- Icons: simple line icons or filled minimal
- Animations: subtle fade-in on scroll, no heavy motion
- Interactive demo: dark card with dropdown, instant result reveal
- Report preview: styled like a real PDF/dashboard card with sections
